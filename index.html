A Good Girl's Guide to Murder- Sun Apr 9

I just finished my book: A Good Girl's Guide to Murder. My favorite part about this book was that the plot wasn't obvious unlike most of the other mystery books I have read and that I could relate to the main character a lot. The main character, Pippa, is a senior at her high school who decides to solve her town's darkest crime that happened 5 years ago for a school project. I like that her personality and characteristics enhances the plot to its best.



I stayed up all night to finish this book.
